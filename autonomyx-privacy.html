Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2024

1. Introduction

Autonomyx ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account registration data (name, email, password)
  • Profile information (company, title, team size)
  • Code and code review data submitted to our service
  • Communication data (support tickets, emails, chat)
  • Payment information (processed by Stripe, we don't store)

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • IP address and device information
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Usage patterns and feature preferences
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

2.3 Third-Party Data

We may receive information about you from GitHub, GitLab, or other integrations you authorize.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:

  • Providing and improving our services
  • Processing payments and sending related notices
  • Sending promotional communications (with consent)
  • Responding to support requests
  • Analyzing usage patterns and trends
  • Detecting and preventing fraud or abuse
  • Complying with legal obligations

4. How We Protect Your Data

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data in transit
  • AES-256 encryption for data at rest
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Secure data centers with SOC 2 Type II compliance

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information:

  • With service providers (hosting, analytics, payment processing)
  • With your consent or at your direction
  • For legal requirements or law enforcement
  • To protect our rights and prevent fraud
  • In case of merger, acquisition, or bankruptcy

6. Your Rights

6.1 Access and Portability

You have the right to request access to your personal data and receive it in a portable format.

6.2 Correction and Deletion

You can request correction of inaccurate data. You may also request deletion, subject to legal retention requirements.

6.3 Opt-Out

You can opt out of marketing communications by clicking "unsubscribe" or adjusting your preferences.

6.4 Exercising Your Rights

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@autonomyx.com with your request.

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Authenticate your session
  • Analyze site usage
  • Improve user experience

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Some functionality may be limited if cookies are disabled.

8. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services which may collect data:

  • Analytics: Google Analytics
  • Payment: Stripe
  • Hosting: Google Cloud Run
  • Monitoring: Sentry

These providers have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them.

9. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to provide services or comply with legal obligations. Account data is retained for 90 days after account deletion unless legally required otherwise.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to, processed, and stored in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have data protection laws different from your home country. By using Autonomyx, you consent to such transfers.

11. GDPR and CCPA Compliance

11.1 For EU Residents (GDPR)

We process data lawfully under GDPR, including processing for contract fulfillment and legitimate interests. You have rights including access, rectification, erasure, and data portability.

11.2 For California Residents (CCPA)

You have rights to know, delete, and opt-out of data sales. We do not sell personal information.

12. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete it promptly.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be announced via email or prominent notice on our site. Continued use constitutes acceptance of changes.

14. Contact Us

For privacy questions or requests:

  • Email: privacy@autonomyx.com
  • Address: Autonomyx Inc., San Francisco, CA
  • Response time: 30 days or as required by law
