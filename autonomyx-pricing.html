Frequently Asked Questions

  • Can I change my plan anytime? ⌄

    Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle. If you upgrade mid-month, we'll prorate the difference.

  • What happens after my free trial ends? ⌄

    Your 14-day free trial is completely free—no credit card required. When it ends, your account converts to the Free plan. You can upgrade to Professional or Enterprise anytime.

  • Do you offer refunds? ⌄

    We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with Autonomyx for any reason, contact our support team and we'll refund your subscription cost.

  • Can I host Autonomyx on my own infrastructure? ⌄

    Yes! Enterprise customers can deploy Autonomyx on-premise. This includes self-hosted and private cloud options. Contact our sales team for details.

  • Do you have volume discounts? ⌄

    Yes! We offer volume discounts for teams purchasing 10+ seats. Contact our sales team at sales@autonomyx.com for a custom quote.

  • What payment methods do you accept? ⌄

    We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), ACH transfers, and wire transfers. Enterprise customers can also arrange custom payment terms.
